chinese mei society

facebook event banners promoting nyu's chinese cultural club events

I designed four playful and eye-catching Facebook event banners to attract NYU students to CMS’s on-campus events. I chose simple yet bold color schemes and graphics so that students would be able to easily recognize and take interest in CMS’s events when scrolling on their Facebook feeds. CMS saw higher online engagement and more consistent event turnout.

- - -

To make banners more visually cohesive, I also adapted the CMS logo to match the theme of each banner.
