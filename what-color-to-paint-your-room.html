what color to paint your room to best complement the colors of the sky

an artist book exploring color theory and the sky

For my Book Arts Design Studio class, I created a conceptual artist book exploring color theory. I love the feeling of looking out of a bedroom window and discovering the ever-changing colors of the sky, so I decided to take images of the sky in various states and select a color of a bedroom wall that would, when juxtaposed, enrich the colors of the sky.

I laser cut out window panes so that readers can see the effect of the color pairings together, and included typography of copy that I wrote myself to provide brief reasonings for my color choices. I also laser cut out the cover to tie the opening page in with the rest of the book.
